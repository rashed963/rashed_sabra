# Gate 2 Wireframes

These files are static grayscale layout studies. They are not a prototype and do not authorize production implementation.

## Open

- `direction-a.html` — Editorial Split
- `direction-b.html` — Centered Human Signal

Open each file directly in a browser. Each page includes fixed-width desktop (`1440px`), tablet (`834px`), and mobile (`390px`) artboards.

## Direction A: Editorial Split

- Hero copy and portrait share the opening screen.
- The belief manifesto is a horizontal chapter.
- Selected thoughts use a stable two-card grid.
- Professional proof is compact and scannable.
- Strength: quickest path to identity and writing.
- Risk: composition may feel more familiar and less singular.

## Direction B: Centered Human Signal

- Portrait and network are the dominant opening anchor.
- Identity and thesis form a centered statement beneath the portrait.
- The manifesto uses an asymmetric editorial split.
- Selected thoughts are staggered and proof becomes a vertical sequence.
- Strength: more personal and visually distinctive.
- Risk: slower first-screen scanning and a longer vertical rhythm.

## Review Boundary

Evaluate:

1. Identity comprehension
2. Portrait prominence
3. CTA visibility
4. Manifesto placement and reading comfort
5. Transition into selected thoughts
6. Mobile stacking
7. Overall pacing

Do not evaluate color, typography personality, photography quality, animation, starfield realism, or final copy polish at this gate.
