# Human-Directed Knowledge Network: Moodboard

## Purpose

Calibrate visual quality and interaction principles without copying another site’s layout or identity.

## Reference Set

### Lusion

- URL: https://lusion.co/
- Borrow:
  - One coherent interactive world rather than unrelated effects
  - High craft in motion, depth, and visual transitions
  - A memorable first impression tied to a clear concept
- Avoid:
  - Agency-showreel density
  - Continuous immersive behavior across every section
  - 3D spectacle that delays identity or reading

### Dennis Snellenberg

- URL: https://www.dennissnellenberg.com/
- Borrow:
  - Immediate personal identity
  - Disciplined hierarchy and concise positioning
  - Clear separation between introduction and proof
- Avoid:
  - Generic freelancer positioning
  - Reusing its layout language as a template

### Tarek Atrissi Design

- URL: https://www.atrissi.com/
- Borrow:
  - Serious treatment of Arabic and Latin typography
  - Respect for Arabic text as a primary design material
  - Contemporary bilingual craft
- Avoid:
  - Calligraphy or type-design studio aesthetics unrelated to Rashed’s identity
  - Decorative Arabic lettering that reduces readability

### modvisual

- URL: https://modvisual.com/
- Borrow:
  - “Living visuals” connected to a readable personal point of view
  - Compact focus areas that bridge creativity and technology
  - Technical capability expressed through selected proof
- Avoid:
  - Project-portfolio density
  - Positioning Rashed as a creative technologist or studio

### Lee Robinson

- URL: https://leerob.io/
- Borrow:
  - Curation over premature taxonomy
  - Short identity statement followed by selected writing
  - Low-friction path into real content
- Avoid:
  - An overly plain visual system for this project

## Direction Words

- Personal
- Intelligent
- Directed
- Trustworthy
- Alive
- Precise
- Calm after impact
- Technically credible

## Visual Ingredients

- Real portrait with recognizable facial detail
- Deep navy field rather than absolute black
- Electric teal paths and nodes
- Restrained warm signal used only for human decisions
- Arabic display typography with quieter Latin technical labels
- Layered depth concentrated around the portrait
- Calm editorial sections below the hero
- Visible whitespace and strong reading order

## Avoid List

- Stock AI brain imagery
- Random circuit-board traces
- Neon cyberpunk overload
- Floating pills around a portrait
- Generic SaaS gradients
- Dense dashboards or terminal decoration
- Glass cards as the dominant visual language
- Motion behind long-form text
- Effects that make Arabic typography harder to read
