# Gate 2 Review: Homepage Wireframes

## Scope

Review only the two grayscale layout directions in `wireframes/`:

- Direction A: Editorial Split
- Direction B: Centered Human Signal

Each direction includes desktop, tablet, and mobile artboards. No production code, polished mockup, motion asset, or prototype has been created.

## Ten-Minute Review

1. Open both HTML files in a browser.
2. Look at each desktop hero for ten seconds.
3. State who the site represents and what the primary next action is.
4. Scroll through the complete desktop composition.
5. Repeat quickly for tablet and mobile.
6. Choose one direction. Do not combine them during the first review.

## Questions

1. Which direction identifies Rashed faster?
2. In which direction is the portrait important without overpowering the thesis?
3. Is `استكشف أفكاري` clearly the primary action?
4. Does `What I Believe` feel like a meaningful worldview or a generic quote section?
5. Does the transition from manifesto to selected writing feel natural?
6. Are the two articles easy to find?
7. Does professional proof support credibility without becoming a résumé?
8. Does Journey remain easy to find?
9. Which mobile composition feels calmer and easier to scan?
10. What is the single strongest concern with the chosen direction?

## Decision

Choose exactly one:

- `APPROVE A`

- `REVISE A`
- `REVISE B`
- `REJECT BOTH`
