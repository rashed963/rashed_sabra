# Bilingual Content Policy

## Primary Rule

The website is Arabic-first.

- Write all navigation, headings, labels, calls to action, descriptions, article prose, and accessibility text in Arabic.
- Do not use English as decorative editorial copy.
- Keep approved English terms in Latin script when they are official titles, credentials, brands, abbreviations, or established industry language.

## Approved English Usage

- `Head of Product & Technology`
- `Product & Technology`
- `AI`
- `NLP`
- `Agentic AI`
- `AI Agent`
- `AI Governance`
- `Software Engineering`
- `Software Development`
- `Robotics Simulation`
- `Delivery Reliability`
- `Technical Strategy`
- `Features`
- `Workflow`
- `Web`
- `CAD`
- `LinkedIn`
- `Notion`
- `MSc in Big Data Systems`
- Official organization and institution names
- Commands, paths, and code values

## Consistency Rules

1. Use the approved English form exactly; do not alternate with an Arabic translation elsewhere.
2. Keep surrounding grammar and explanation in Arabic.
3. Use Arabic for generic concepts that have clear equivalents, such as المنتج, الجودة, التسليم, القرار, الملكية, والحوكمة العامة.
4. Use `Product & Technology` as one fixed industry phrase. Use المنتج when referring only to a product.
5. Use `Software Engineering` for the discipline and `Software Development` only when specifically referring to development work.
6. Use `AI Governance` as the fixed technical term. Use الحوكمة when discussing governance generically.
7. Pure-English sections are not permitted unless the site later introduces an explicit language switch.

## Examples

- Correct: `أقود Product & Technology لبناء أنظمة AI موثوقة.`
- Correct: `تجمع خبرتي بين Software Engineering وNLP.`
- Incorrect: `What I Believe`
- Incorrect: `قيادة المنتج والتقنية` in one section and `Product & Technology` for the same concept in another.
- Incorrect: `هندسة البرمجيات` in one milestone and `Software Engineering` in another.
